Power BI Sales Dashboard

This project is an **interactive Sales Dashboard built in Power BI**.  
It allows business users to explore sales data easily and make informed decisions by analyzing trends, top products, and regional performance.

Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to help business teams quickly understand:

- Total revenue, profit, and units sold
- Performance of top-selling products and countries
- Monthly sales trends and seasonal patterns
- Customer segmentation insights

This dashboard can be used by sales managers, executives, and analysts to make data-driven decisions.

Features

- **Total Sales, Profit, Profit % & Units Sold** – Key performance indicators at a glance.
- **Top-Selling Products & Countries** – Quickly identify what drives revenue.
- **Sales by Segment & Customer** – Understand which customer segments contribute most.
- **Month-on-Month Sales Trend** – Track growth or decline over time.
- **Interactive Filters (Year & Month)** – Drill down into specific periods.
- **Custom Theme & Background** – Improves readability and visual appeal.

Dataset

- **Source:** Practice Excel file (`SalesData.xlsx`)
- **Format:** Tabular sales data including Date, Product, Country, Segment, Units Sold, Sales, Profit
- **Notes:** Data is anonymized for practice purposes

How to Use

1. Download `SalesDashboard.pbix` and `SalesData.xlsx`.
2. Open `SalesDashboard.pbix` in Power BI Desktop.
3. Use slicers to filter by Year or Month.
4. Explore visuals for insights.
5. Optional: see screenshots in the `screenshots/` folder for reference.
